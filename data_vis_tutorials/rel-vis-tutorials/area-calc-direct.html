Fixed area visualizer: Direct manipulation

The values x, y, and area exist in relationship. Manipulating one value impacts the others.

Here, alter the values by typing or by dragging the top or right edge of the blue box.

x * y = area

This is a more complex application of the Drag/Resize SVG demo. Click here to see the tutorial on that.

The difference is that there are more calculations involved, thus more variables to declare, but the math is relatively simple to work out.
